Serverless Distributed Scan

"ClickHouse Cloud is where your data lives, chDB is what your agent thinks with, and Lambda MicroVMs is where it gets to think — in private." Launch N Firecracker microVMs (each a private chDB), suspend to $0, resume snapshot-hot, then scatter shards of a public S3 dataset — e.g. ~2.5 billion Overture buildings — at every engine's /scan and merge. Nothing is ingested or copied; the data is read straight from S3.

⚠ Illustrative single run — not a benchmark. No warm-up, no repeated trials, no controlled network; numbers move run to run. The robust claims are the shape: $0 compute at rest, per-request isolation, per-second billing.
